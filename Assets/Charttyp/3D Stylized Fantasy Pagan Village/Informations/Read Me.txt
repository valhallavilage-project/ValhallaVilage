This is pack of drag and drop prefabs.

There are some hanging objects like lantern_hanging, torch_hanging, leafs, ropes or 
decorations objects and because of that pivot points of these objects is not in bottom center.

Roofs don't have pivot points in bottom center too, because thakns to that it's easier to connect them with walls.

Wall_dirt have pivot point in their center because thaks to that it's easier to put them on the walls.

There are objects like rope_tide, fence_picket, fence_branches, fence_branches_pole. I made this objects 
as a separate, single meshes to be used in other 3D software like Blender. Thanks to that You have
prepared meshes to make Your own objects.
